Majal albenaa contracting co.

Established in 1988

A leading company in the field of general contracting in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. Since its establishment, the company has achieved great success in executing many major projects in the Kingdom, including projects for hospitals, schools, commercial markets, and government entities. The company has an impressive track record of experience and achievements, earning it a good reputation in the contracting field. The company is committed to providing high-quality contracting services that meet customer requirements, as well as Saudi safety and quality standards. The Construction Field Contracting Company aims to be the preferred partner for government and private entities in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia by delivering high-quality contracting services that meet customer requirements and contribute to the development of the Saudi economy.
